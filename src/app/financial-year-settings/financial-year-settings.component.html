Financial year settings

Please set month and day of the year to mark when financial year starts. e.g. April 1st. It must be noted that by changing the month and day settings, system will update the financial years for projects which are already entered as per new settings and may drop disbursements that are out of the span of the financial years assigned to projects. It is recommended to take backup of the database before applying new settings so that in any other case AIMS may be reverted to the current state of the database.
